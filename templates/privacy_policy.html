Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to HealthCare Plus Hospital Management System. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our hospital management system.

By registering and using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We respect your privacy rights and comply with applicable data protection laws.

Important: Your medical information is confidential and protected under healthcare privacy regulations. We take data security seriously.

Information We Collect

  • Basic Details: Name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, physical address
  • Emergency Contact: Name and phone number of emergency contact person
  • Identification: Username for system access

  • Blood Group: For emergency and treatment purposes
  • Medical History: Previous treatments, diagnoses, and prescriptions
  • Appointment Records: Booking details, visit dates, and appointment status
  • Treatment Notes: Doctor's observations and recommendations

  • Login Credentials: Username (stored as plain text)
  • Password: Encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
  • Session Data: Temporary session cookies for authentication

  • Login Activity: Login timestamps and frequency
  • Account Status: Active/inactive status
  • Browser Information: Browser type and device information (for compatibility)

How We Use Your Information

Appointment Management

Schedule, confirm, and manage your medical appointments with our healthcare professionals.

Medical Records

Maintain accurate and accessible medical history for quality healthcare delivery.

Communication

Send appointment reminders, updates, and important health-related notifications.

Security

Prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and ensure the safety of our platform.

Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Password Encryption

All passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing algorithm with 12 rounds of salting.

Secure Database

Data stored in SQLite database with proper access permissions and regular backups.

Session Security

Session-based authentication with secure cookies and automatic timeout.

HTTPS Encryption

In production, all data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocol.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

Your information is shared only when necessary and in limited circumstances:

Who What Information Purpose
Assigned Doctors Your medical history and appointment details To provide medical care and treatment
Hospital Administration Appointment records and basic information System management and operational purposes
Legal Authorities As required by law Legal compliance, court orders, or public safety
We DO NOT: Share your data with third-party advertisers, sell your information, or use it for marketing purposes without consent.

Your Privacy Rights

As a user of our system, you have the following rights:

Access

View and download your personal and medical data anytime

Correct

Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile

Delete

Request deletion of your account and associated data

Cookies & Tracking

Our system uses minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies to maintain your login session (deleted when you logout or close browser)
  • Remember Me: Optional persistent cookie if you check "Remember Me" during login (expires after 30 days)
  • No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking scripts

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect system functionality.
