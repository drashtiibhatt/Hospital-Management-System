General Questions

The Hospital Management System is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline healthcare operations. It allows patients to book appointments online, access their medical records, and manage their healthcare journey digitally. Doctors can manage their schedules, view patient history, and provide treatment records efficiently.

The system is designed for three types of users:
  • Patients: Anyone can register as a patient to book appointments and access medical records
  • Doctors: Healthcare professionals added by hospital administration
  • Administrators: Hospital staff managing the system and operations

In production, all communications are encrypted using HTTPS. Your medical information is confidential and only accessible to you and your assigned doctors.

The system works best on modern browsers including Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience. The system is also mobile-friendly and works on smartphones and tablets.

Registration & Login

  1. Click on the "Register" button on the home page
  2. Fill in the registration form with your personal details (name, email, phone, date of birth, etc.)
  3. Choose a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
  4. Submit the form
  5. You can now login with your username and password

Required information includes:
  • Full name
  • Username (unique)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Address
  • Blood group (optional but recommended)
  • Emergency contact number

Booking Appointments

  1. Login to your patient account
  2. Click on "Find Doctors" in the navigation menu
  3. Search by specialization or doctor name
  4. Click "View Profile" on your chosen doctor
  5. Click "Book Appointment"
  6. Select your preferred date and time slot
  7. Add reason for visit (optional)
  8. Click "Confirm Booking"
  9. You'll receive instant confirmation!

Yes! You can search for doctors by specialization and view their profiles, including qualifications, experience, consultation fees, and patient reviews. You can then choose any doctor based on your preference and their availability.

When you click "Book Appointment" on a doctor's profile, you'll see their available time slots for the next 7 days. Available slots are shown in green. Simply select a date to see the time slots for that day, then choose your preferred time.

Go to "My Appointments" → Find the appointment you want to cancel → Click "Cancel Appointment" → Confirm cancellation. Note: Please cancel at least 24 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees.

  • 24+ hours notice: Free cancellation
  • Less than 24 hours: 50% cancellation fee
  • No-show: Full consultation fee charged
  • 3+ no-shows: Account may be temporarily suspended

Yes, you can book appointments with different doctors or for different dates. However, you cannot book two appointments with the same doctor at the same time. The system prevents double-booking automatically.

Treatment & Medical Records

Login to your patient account and click on "Treatment History" in the navigation menu. You'll see a complete timeline of all your past treatments, including diagnoses, prescriptions, and doctor's notes.

Yes! On the treatment history page, you can print or save each treatment record as a PDF using your browser's print function. This allows you to keep offline copies or share them with other healthcare providers.

Medical records are retained indefinitely as per healthcare regulations. Even if you delete your account, essential medical records may be retained for legal and medical archiving purposes.

For Doctors

Doctor accounts are created by hospital administrators. You'll receive your login credentials (username and password) from the admin. Use these credentials on the login page to access your doctor dashboard.

Login → Click "Availability" in the navigation → Add available time slots for each day → Click "Save Availability". You can set your schedule for the next 7 days, which will be visible to patients when they book appointments.

Go to "Appointments" → Click on the appointment → Enter diagnosis, prescription, and notes → Click "Complete Appointment". The appointment status will change to "Completed" and the treatment record will be saved to the patient's history.

Payment & Fees

Consultation fees vary by doctor and specialization, typically ranging from ₹500 to ₹1000. The exact fee is displayed on each doctor's profile before you book an appointment.

Payment is typically made at the hospital reception after your consultation. The system is free to use - there are no subscription fees or charges for booking appointments online.
