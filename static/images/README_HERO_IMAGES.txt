HERO CAROUSEL IMAGES GUIDE
===========================

Please add 3 images to this folder (static/images/) for the home page carousel:

IMAGE 1: hero-slide-1.jpg
--------------------------
Theme: Healthcare/Hospital Building or Patient Care
Suggested content:
- Modern hospital building exterior
- Doctor consulting with patient
- Medical team in hospital corridor
Dimensions: 1920x1080px (recommended)
Format: JPG or PNG


IMAGE 2: hero-slide-2.jpg
--------------------------
Theme: Doctors/Medical Professionals
Suggested content:
- Group of doctors/medical professionals
- Doctor with stethoscope
- Medical team in scrubs
Dimensions: 1920x1080px (recommended)
Format: JPG or PNG


IMAGE 3: hero-slide-3.jpg
--------------------------
Theme: Medical Technology/Appointments
Suggested content:
- Patient using digital healthcare app/tablet
- Modern medical equipment
- Doctor and patient booking appointment
Dimensions: 1920x1080px (recommended)
Format: JPG or PNG


WHERE TO FIND FREE MEDICAL IMAGES:
-----------------------------------
1. Unsplash.com - Search for "hospital", "doctor", "medical"
2. Pexels.com - Search for "healthcare", "medical team"
3. Pixabay.com - Search for "hospital building", "doctor patient"

IMPORTANT NOTES:
----------------
- Images should be high quality (at least 1920x1080px)
- Ensure images are free to use (check licenses)
- Images with people on the left/center work best (text appears on left)
- Bright, professional images work better than dark images
- The overlay will darken the image, so slightly bright images are ideal

FALLBACK:
---------
If images are not added, the carousel will show gradient backgrounds as fallback.
